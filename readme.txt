Gutty Related Posts is the best way to show related and relevant content on your WordPress site with the Block Editor, Full Site Editing or shortcode.

== Description ==

Gutty Related Posts is a powerful and easy-to-use WordPress plugin that leverages the power of Full Site Editing (FSE) and the Block Editor to display highly relevant related posts on your website. Using TD-IDF (Term Frequency-Inverse Document Frequency) technology, the plugin analyzes your content to provide the most contextually relevant posts, enhancing user engagement and keeping visitors on your site longer.

<h3>Key Features</h3>

- <strong>Relevant Post Suggestions</strong>: Automatically suggest related posts based on content analysis using TD-IDF, ensuring readers see posts that matter most.
- <strong>Full Site Editing Integration</strong>: Built to work seamlessly with WordPress' Full Site Editing, allowing you to customize the look and feel of the related posts section anywhere on your site.
- <strong>Easy Customization</strong>: Use the block editor to fully customize the display of related posts. Show or hide post elements like titles, thumbnails, and excerpts with simple checkboxes.
- <strong>No Ads, 100% Free</strong>: Enjoy a completely ad-free experience with no hidden fees. Gutty Related Posts is free and always will be.
- <strong>Optimized for Performance</strong>: Lightweight and efficient, ensuring fast page loads without compromising on functionality.

<h3>Compatibility</h3>
- Fully compatible with WordPress Full Site Editing (FSE).
- Optimized for use with the Block Editor (Gutenberg).
- Also works with any page builders supporting shortcodes.

Make your content more engaging by offering visitors personalized, relevant reading options. Install Gutty Related Posts today!

Source available on our Github public repository: https://github.com/guttycorp/gutty-related-posts-public

== Installation ==

1. Upload 'gutty-related-posts' to the '/wp-content/plugins/' directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Click on Settings, Gutty Related Posts, click Generate related posts button.
4. Go to the Editor (Block Editor or Full Site Editing), add the Related Posts block, edit the settings. Or use the [gutty_related_posts] shortcode from your page builder.
5. Enjoy!
